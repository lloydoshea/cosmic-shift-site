Privacy Policy

Cosmic Shift respects your privacy.

This app may display advertisements provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers, usage data, coarse location information, crash data, and performance data. This information is used to serve advertisements and measure ad performance.

Cosmic Shift does not create user accounts and does not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or precise location data.

The app does not implement App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and does not request tracking permission from users. Ads served may be non-personalized depending on device settings and Apple’s policies.

In-app purchases (such as Remove Ads and Starter Pack) are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. Cosmic Shift does not store or process payment information.
